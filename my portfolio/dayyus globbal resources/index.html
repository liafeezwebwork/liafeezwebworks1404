Loading Process from Quarry

Efficient loading and dispatch of granite aggregates across Nigeria

How We Load Granite Aggregate

  1. Excavation: Bulldozers and excavators dig out the granite material from the quarry.
  2. Crushing: Raw granite is crushed into different sizes using crushing plants.
  3. Loading: Front loaders or excavators scoop and load granite into dump trucks.
  4. Weighing: Each loaded truck is weighed for proper documentation.
  5. Dispatch: Trucks are then dispatched to client sites or storage yards.
